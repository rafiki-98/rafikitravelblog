Tips for your first time in Costa Rica

The weather in Costa Rica can change in a moment. You can have heavy rain and a few hours later be the best day of your life, with a sunny day and a rainbow in the horizon. Costa Rica is not just beach so don't just bring your shorts and t-shirts!

If you are from a country where breakfast is small, you would feel Costa Rica's breakfast is HUGE. In Costa Rica we take very seriously that breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and probably that's why we start the day with rice and beans, combined with eggs, sour cream and tortillas. How do you call this? Gallo Pinto
