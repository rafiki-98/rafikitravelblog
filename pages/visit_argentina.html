Welcome to the land of Diego and Lionel

Argentina, Argentina... Is impossible not to get excited when you think about Argentina. The country fundamentals are based in migration, back then and now. Probably one of the reasons I migrated to this country was how you are treated by most of the people. Argentineans are very kind and they always want to learn more about you and your culture.

Tips for your first time in Argentina

Argentina's inflation is terrible, but if you come to Argentina with dollars in hand, you would probably feel as the richest person of the world. So never change with your card or in the airport, because you might be losing up to 75% just in taxes!

You would not believe me until you see it by yourself, but, Buenos Aires is probably one of the best cities in the world. Gastronomy from so many different cultures, the architecture is really close to any European city and you can also see how the modern Buenos Aires is combined with the old one. Music is everywhere, people singing or playing instruments in the Subway, concerts around the city all the time, if I ever tell you what is going on here, I would never finish talking.

Argentina literally has 24 provinces and is one of the biggest countries in the world. The country has a lot to offer in terms of nature and landscapes, and you can decide either you want to go to a cold place with snow or if you prefer to drink wine in the feet of Los Andes.
